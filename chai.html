The Journey of Chai

From ancient traditions to modern innovation, follow the aromatic path of chai through time and culture.

Ancient Origins

The story of chai begins over 5,000 years ago in the royal courts of India and Siam, where it was used as an ayurvedic beverage to heal and energize.

The Spice Route

As the spice trade flourished, chai's recipe evolved, incorporating exotic spices like cardamom, cinnamon, and cloves from around the world.

British Influence

The British introduced tea plantations to India in the 1830s, forever changing the relationship between chai and its homeland.

Global Phenomenon

Today, chai has captivated the world, bringing its complex flavors and aromatic warmth to every corner of the globe, while still honoring its rich heritage.
